Leonardo da Vinci Tribute

Arquetipo y símbolo del hombre del Renacimiento, genio universal, además de filósofo humanista.
Posible Autorretrato de Leonardo da Vinci hecho entre 1512 y 1515.

    Here's a time line of Leonardo's life:

  • 1452 Born in Tuscan, Republic of Florence.
  • 1469 Entered as apprentice to prestigious art workshop under the teaching of Andrea del Verrochio.
  • 1473 Paisaje de Santa Maria della neve, a pen and ink drawing.
  • 1472-1475 La Anunciación.
  • 1478 He offered to build the octagonal church of San Juan de Florencia.
  • 1483-1486 La Virgen de las Rocas.
  • 1490 He created an academy named like him.
  • 1493 He was sent to Pavi with the title << ingéniarius ducalis >>
  • 1494-1498 La ultima cena.
  • 1498 Build the roof of the castle of the Sforza.
  • 1501 La Virgen y el Niño con santa Ana y san Juan Bautista
  • 1503-1505 La batalla de Anghiari.
  • 1503-1506 and 1510-1515 La Gioconda.
  • 1505 Codex on the Flight of Birds
  • 1507 Leonardo was as the sole heir of his uncle Francesco.
  • 1508 lived with the sculptor Giovanni Francesco Rústica at Piero di Braccio Martelli's house, in Florence.
  • 1513 Lived in Roma, where work for the papa Leon X.
  • 1514 Made "Diluvios", in bóveda de la Capilla Sixtina.
  • 1519 Sick for several months, he wrote his will before a notary of Amboise.
  • 1519 He Die on May 2, with 67 years old.

"LEONARDUS VINCIUS: QUID PLURA? DIVINUM INGENIUM, DIVINA MANUS, EMORI IN SINU REGIO MERUERE. VIRTUS ET FORTUNA HOC MONUMENTUM CONTINGERE GRAVISSIMIS IMPENSIS CURAVERUNT"

"Leonardo de Vinci, what else can you say? His divine genius and his divine hand deserved to expire on the chest of a king. Virtue and fortune veil, a prize for great expenses, in this monument that corresponds to it."

-- Giorgio Vasari.
